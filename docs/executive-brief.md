# Executive Brief – Lulu-Waste Kuwait

## Overview

Lulu-Waste Kuwait is a strategic ESG and CSR initiative designed to
address household plastic waste while strengthening Lulu Hypermarket’s
sustainability profile.

## Positioning

The initiative positions Lulu as a CSR Enabler rather than a waste
management operator.

All operational activities are conducted by licensed contractors
such as WASCO under Kuwait’s regulatory framework.

## Key Outcomes

- Increased PET recovery
- Improved ESG indicators
- Enhanced customer engagement
- Investor-grade reporting metrics
